Megaline — Plan Revenue Analysis

Analytical case study comparing two mobile plans using user behavior and plan rules to support a pricing/business decision. The focus is on clean assumptions, clear metrics, and defensible conclusions.

Context & goal

Compare two plans and determine whether one generates significantly more revenue than the other, based on customer usage patterns.

Approach

  • Data prep: cleaned usage tables, validated joins, and created monthly user-level aggregates
  • Business logic: implemented plan rules to compute monthly revenue per user
  • Analysis: compared distributions and summarized key drivers
  • Statistics: used hypothesis testing (Welch's t-test) to compare plan revenue

Key findings

  • Built a monthly revenue model by customer using plan rules and usage behavior (calls, texts, data).
  • Compared revenue distributions across plans and summarized key usage drivers behind the differences.
  • Used statistical testing (Welch's t-test) to support a defensible recommendation.
