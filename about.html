About

I’m building this portfolio to document my growth toward a future Data Scientist role. My projects focus on the full workflow: turning messy datasets into clear insights, building baselines, and communicating results in a way that’s useful for decision-making.

My background is in business operations and cross-functional support, which shaped how I approach data work: define the question, choose the right metric, validate assumptions, and deliver something actionable. I care about clarity, reproducibility, and thoughtful trade-offs—not just “getting a model to run.”

This site is a living log of what I’m learning and improving. As I complete new sprints, I update older work (cleaner notebooks, better evaluation, clearer storytelling) and keep my best projects easy to explore.
